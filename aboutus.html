About Eduraaga

Live Tutor Education services

The DNA of the organization is based on practitioners who practice what they preach enabled byempirical knowledge gained over several years of learning and teaching. Carnatic Vocal music iscore to the culture of India and is a very important skill for the Indian Diaspora across the world.EduRaaga aims to polish the Carnatic / Light Music credentials accompanied by the best in classVoice Training. We provide a unique opportunity for students achieve the greater level of proficiencyby enabling real time digital access to the celebrities and leveraging superior content on our Digitalplatform.
EduRaaga is propelled by the Power of superstars in Carnatic & Light Music along with renownedexpertise on Voice management being available to tutor the aspiring students. The EduRaagaProgram is enabled by a structured curriculum, a growth path, live tutor interactions (Digital & Faceto Face) and powered by our superior digital platform. Our roadmap provides for gradual transition ofthe curriculum to include Hindustani & Bollywood Music as well very soon.

Our Partners

KALALAYA was founded in 1992 with a mission of promoting Indian arts andculture in the state of California. Now in existence for over 30 years, KALALAYA has become one of the premier world music institutions not only in California,but across the United States of America. In addition to presenting premier Classical Carnatic music, dance and light music programs by celebrated artistsof India, KALALAYA ‘s goal is to also introduce Indian arts, crafts and culture, tochildren through lectures, demonstrations by visiting artists, workshops andsummer heritage camp. The distinct feature of this organization is its mission to reach out to the local community and bring both entertainment and education/outreach of Indian arts and culture to the residents of the State of California.
