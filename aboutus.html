About Eduraaga

THE COMPANY

The DNA of the organization is based on practitioners who practice what they preach enabled by empirical knowledge gained over several years of learning and teaching. Carnatic Vocal music is core to the culture of India and is a very important skill for the Indian Diaspora across the world. Digipropel EduRaaga aims to polish the Carnatic credentials and bridge the transition to light music for which Carnatic music forms the base. We provide a unique opportunity for students achieve the greater level of proficiency and transition to Light Music leveraging superior content on the platform.
EduRaaga is propelled by the Power of superstars in Carnatic & Light Music along with renowned expertise on Voice management being available to tutor the aspiring students. The EduRaaga Program is enabled by a structured curriculum, a growth path, live tutor interactions and powered by a learning management solution. Our roadmap provides for gradual transition of the curriculum to include Hindustani Music as well very soon.

01.

WHO WE ARE

Digipropel EduRaaga is a division of Digipropel focused on bringing traditional Indian music education transitioning into Light Music with a celebrity led team. Digipropel is a Bay Area based organization focused on digital disruption of the status quo. Our program content has been developed by academicians and practitioners with several years of expertise. DigipropelTMEdu strategy focuses on the why part of learning a solution and enables a focus on the goal of achieving the best understanding of the program.

02.

OUR STANDARDS

Digipropel EduRaaga is focused on bring the highest level of quality into the Music Education ecosystem in terms of content, technology, celebrity involvement or data privacy. Our management team monitors every aspect of the aforementioned and will be monitoring the programs on a daily basis to identify areas for continuous improvement. We are committed to deliver the best to ensure value.

03.

WHY CHOOSE US

Carnatic and Hindustani Music are the essence of excellence in Light Music and its imperative that the right tutors with the right blend of celebrity and voice expert involvement is part of the program. Digipropel EduRaaga has ensured the same by bringing in the right celebrities to anchor the programs and support from the tutors. This Signature program is one of its kind being introduced into the ecosystem by Digipropel.
