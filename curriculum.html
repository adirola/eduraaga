Our Curriculum:

EduRaaga as the name signifies is oriented towards taking advanced levels of Indian music to the world through our team of celebrated Celebrities on our live interactive digital platform. Celebrity led Music tutoring on a global scale would not be possible otherwise. Certification to the students would be provided as well.

Carnatic

The Basic, Advanced and Advanced + Celebrity Only Carnatic programs span over 10 week modules each. Programs is designed to bring out the professional artist in the student and helps in learning heavy compositions with a lot of scope for improvisations. It covers from Keerthanai, Manodharma Sangeetham, Raagam Neraval Swaram and Raagam Thaanam Pallavi.
In this progressive curriculum, different famous carnatic compositions will be taught by legendary singers after all evaluation processes. Your nuances, classical knowledge, voice structure and culture, knowledge about different Carnatic compositions of different composers all will be checked and evaluated during audition. Based on all these above said parameters, your learning level will be fixed as basic, advanced, advanced plus.
Carnatic Course Prerequisites: Minimum knowledge of Varnam required. It will not be a regular Carnatic teaching session and it will be a journey to different folds in a with wide exposure from top rated legendary singers. Song wise coverage would be enabled across bhavam, ragam, thaanam and other nuances. Celebrity Signed certificates would be made available for proficient students

Light Music Track

The Light Music Track is a specialized Celebrity Led Signature Program in modules of 10 weeks across Basic, Advanced and Advanced + where the student's intensive involvement in light music singing happens. This course will be anchored by the one or more of the core celebrities from our panel with Orchestra support from remote studio. There will be an opportunity for a face to face interaction and for singing alongside the celebrity periodically.

Through this online live interactive course, one will be able to:

  • Appreciate and align with fundamental concepts like the tune, beat, and tempo of singing.
  • Experience the nuances of film songs such as using the right pitch, tune, beat and tempo for the context and Entry/ Exit at the right beats with the right tempo.
  • Sing few specific Tamil / Telugu/ Kannada/ Malayalam Film songs applying the correct music principles.
  • The program will also focus on correct pronunciation/correction and in identifying the suitable pitch/scale for the singers.

Voice Training Track

Voice training is based on a method sport-based model wherein the Singer is trained to become aware of ones singing 'self'. The training approach enables the student to holistically express his / her total self in songs that are totally within their current capacity. As the expression blows out with freedom and abandon, the ambit of the singer's 'capacity' rapidly expands. A certification from Celebrity and Company will be provided at end of program. Our theme centers on the fact that Everyone has a right to sing beautifully.
All students go through the same rigor - one which just cajoles the singing out of their voices. Ananth switches from a breezy S.P. Balasubramanian number to a tanam in the Carnatic raga Dhanyasi, then from an Asha Bhosle cabaret song to a Bade Ghulam Ali Khan thumri with the ease of a trapeze artist. "Music is within us. We are music. We have to discover the songs within our voices. That is the exhilarating part." And it is this exhilaration that Ananth is so passionate about sharing. He sees in every simple singer a great vocal possibility and he craves to see that disbelief and joy on the faces of people when they suddenly find themselves doing things that seemed impossible so far. The minimum qualification for his training program in Bangalore is the ability to pick up at least simple tunes. That's all. No knowledge of swaras, no prior training, no experience in performance - just a deep and intense desire to sing better. Reality show aspirants do join but Ananth prefers to train people who are able to think, understand and take charge of their growth.

Voice Training Format: Outside India - Face to Face workshops followed by Digital Training
